Valtio is your digital safe for managing warranties, invoices and purchase documents. Never lose a receipt or forget a warranty expiration date again.

<b>Features:</b>
• Product registration with warranty tracking
• Document attachment (photos and PDFs)
• Automatic warranty calculation
• Customizable alerts (90, 30, 7 days, last day)
• Dashboard with summary
• Search and filters
• Export to PDF and CSV
• Multi-language support (7 languages)

<b>100% Free and Open Source.</b> No ads, no tracking, no data collection.
